Welcome to my "Hang Man - game!

The game is simple:
You have a hidden word. Your task is to use the letters underneath to guess the word.
If you correctly guess all the letters of the hidden word, you win!
However! If you guess the wrong letter too many times, the man dies, and you lose the game.
Good Luck! PS. The chosen category are Australian animals.
